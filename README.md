# Customer Purchase Pattern Analyzer

## Objective
Analyze customer purchase transactions to identify buying patterns, customer segments, revenue drivers, and retention opportunities for a retail/e-commerce business.

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional purchase records with customer demographics, product details, channel/payment information, loyalty status, and purchase dates.

**Key columns:**
- Customer: ID, Name, Age, Gender, City, Region, Occupation, Segment, Loyalty
- Transaction: Purchase Date, Quantity, Unit Price, Total Purchase Value, Discount Used
- Commerce: Payment Method, Purchase Channel
- Recency/Frequency: Purchase Frequency, Last Purchase Date

## Methodology
1. Data Collection (CSV transactional dataset)
2. Data Cleaning & Validation (types, duplicates, missing values, standardization)
3. Feature Engineering (Age Group, Month, Recency metrics)
4. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
5. Customer Segmentation (value and frequency-based views)
6. Trend Analysis (monthly/seasonal patterns)
7. Dashboarding (Power BI/Tableau) + business recommendations

## Analytics Performed
- KPIs: Total Revenue, AOV, Repeat Purchase Rate, Avg Spend per Customer, Basic CLV
- Revenue breakdown by Segment, Category, Region, Channel, Payment Method
- Top customers and purchase frequency distribution
- Seasonal trends using Purchase Date

## Key Insights (examples)
- Identified top revenue-contributing customers and segments (Pareto-style analysis).
- Highlighted high-performing categories and regions for targeted growth.
- Detected retention opportunities using repeat purchase behavior and recency patterns.
- Compared channel performance (Store vs Website vs Mobile App).
- High-value customers and loyalty members contribute a significant share of revenue—prioritize VIP offers and retention.
- Identify “at-risk” customers (high spend but low recency) and run win-back campaigns with personalized bundles.
- Top product categories drive revenue—optimize inventory and promote cross-sell bundles.
- Improve underperforming regions/channels with targeted promotions and better customer experience.
- Win-back email coupon for Gold/Silver with 31–60 days inactivity
- Bundle offers based on top categories
- App push notification reminders for young professionals
- Loyalty upgrade incentive for high AOV customers
